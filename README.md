# Walking Explorers

Where curiosity meets the city.

NYC pilot — AI walking-discovery app. Personalized walking routes through Instagram-worthy cafés, vintage shops, and hidden corners — generated from your tastes, your time, and your real GPS.

## What's deployed here

- `/` — marketing landing page (`index.html`)
- `/beta` — interactive prototype with 12 hand-curated NYC routes (coming soon, see `beta/index.html`)

## Tech

Static HTML, no build step. Deployed via Vercel. Domain: walkingexplorers.com.
